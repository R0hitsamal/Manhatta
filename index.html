Our Story

New York Elevated

Manhatta is a modern New York restaurant, inspired by the unmatched energy of our city. Perched sixty floors above the financial district, we offer a perspective on dining that is as breathtaking as the skyline itself.

Our ever-evolving menu draws on traditional techniques and global influences, bringing unexpected depth to two distinct experiences — our intimate dining room and our vibrant bar & lounge.

Every plate is a celebration of local ingredients, transformed through a culinary approach that feels both familiar and inventive. We believe that great food is inseparable from great place.

Private Dining & Events

Celebrate Above the City

Manhatta is available for full-space buyouts, intimate private dinners, weddings, and company retreats. We offer a variety of flexible sky-high spaces, each designed with celebration in mind.

Our events team will work with you to craft a bespoke experience — from custom menus and curated wine pairings to floral arrangements and personalized service.

  • Intimate private dining rooms
